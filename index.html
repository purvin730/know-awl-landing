People Hub

Manage teams, contracts and communications in one place.

Cost tracking

Real-time budget vs. actual comparison across all jobs. Flag overruns the moment they emerge, not at closeout.

Risk flagging

AI monitors permit timelines, weather windows, and supply lead times to surface risks before they stall a job.

Schedule intelligence

Detect crew conflicts and cascade delays before they happen. Get automated alerts and re-schedule suggestions.

Document AI

Upload RFIs, submittals, and specs. Ask questions in plain English—Know Awl reads the docs so your team doesn't have to.

AI bid estimation

Generate accurate, detailed estimates in minutes. Train the model on your past jobs for bids that match your margins.
